About Us

In 2020, Tyron Slack founded All City Hotdogs in Seattle. We offer a  variety of delicious hotdogs with different toppings and sauces to choose from, as well as a range of sides and drinks.
The mobile hotdog stand is committed to using high-quality, locally sourced ingredients whenever possible and strives to provide a delicious and affordable dining option for locals and tourists alike.

View Our Menu

Mission Statement

We are committed to providing our customers with high-quality food and exceptional service at All City Hotdogs. Our mission is to create a welcoming atmosphere where everyone can enjoy delicious hotdogs made with customer satisfaction in mind. We strive to be an integral part of the Seattle community by supporting local businesses, participating in community events, and giving back through charitable donations.

All City Hotdogs FAQ

Where do you source your ingredients?

We are committed to using only locally-sourced ingredients to ensure the freshest and highest quality products.

Are your hotdogs gluten-free?

Yes, we offer gluten-free hotdog options for our customers with dietary restrictions.

Do you have any vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes, we offer vegetarian and vegan hotdog options made with plant-based ingredients.

Can I customize my hotdog order?

Absolutely! We offer a range of toppings and condiments to allow you to personalize your hotdog order to your liking.

What are your payment options?

We accept cash, credit cards, and mobile payment options like Apple Pay and Google Wallet.

Are you open during inclement weather?

Our hotdog stand is open, rain or shine! We have covered seating available for our customers during inclement weather.
